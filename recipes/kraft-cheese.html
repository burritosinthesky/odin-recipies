Kraft Mackity Mac

Ah, Kraft. The classic, heart-warming cheesy goo slop that your mom made for you in the midst of your depressing youth. It may have been eating away at any chance of muscle development, but it was oh-so tasty.

Ingredients

  • A box of the good stuff (Kraft Cheese)
  • Water
  • Water that's been brought to a roaring boil
  • Time
  • Proper understanding of what it means to enjoy the intricate flavors present in various food groups

Instructions

  1. Put noodles in pot with a roaring boil of water
  2. Eat!
